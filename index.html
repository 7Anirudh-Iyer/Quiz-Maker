Welcome to Flair

At Flair, we make quizzing a joy. We handle all your tasks, from creating to filling in to sending out, to make your experience at flair, pleasurable.

Poetic Ease

In just 5 simple steps, you will have mastered Flair


  1. Scroll all the way down
  2. Click on Create Quiz
  3. Fill in the spaces for question and options
  4. Click the large plus button to add a question
  5. Press Finish Quiz once you're done!
